Company Overview: 
One Eleven is a dynamic and rapidly growing software development and tech consulting company that partners with startups and growing businesses to craft mobile apps, web apps, automations, and systems integrations. We partner with non-technical innovators all over the world to bring their ideas to life and help them simplify and scale their operations. One Eleven prides itself on creating long lasting partnerships and delivering solutions that are simple, effective, and truly impactful.

Job Description: 
Do you want to be part of a team that’s shaping the future of startups worldwide? One Eleven is looking for a Junior Developer to join our small team. This is not just another job - this is your chance to play an instrumental role in the growth and evolution of our company. You’ll be diving into exciting projects, working closely with our team, and having your voice heard as we take on new challenges and opportunities.


We’re looking for someone that enjoys solving problems, learning new skills, and is able to work without supervision with 0 - 3 years of experience. 



You’ll work on real production systems, real clients, and real startup challenges from early on.


Our onboarding process is structured to accelerate your growth:
Month 1: Fully up to speed with our technologies, workflows, and standards
Month 2: Making real contributions to live client projects
Month 3: Building features yourself


You’ll learn fast. You’ll contribute early.
No waiting around in a corporate for years before you’re allowed near production.


If this job post gets you excited and you’ve got the qualifications, don’t hesitate - apply! We value initiative, passion, and drive above all else.


Our office is based in Sandton, Johannesburg, where we work 3 days per week, the other 2 days are work from home. We find that in person collaboration is the best way to develop and expand on skills, while working from home allows you to focus without distractions, making the hybrid system the best strategy overall.


Key Responsibilities:

Collaborate with our team to develop and maintain applications using various frontend and backend technologies.
Work on exciting startup projects, providing technical solutions that are both creative and effective.
Participate in brainstorming sessions, offering your insights and ideas to help shape the direction of projects.
Contribute to code reviews, ensuring that our development practices remain top-notch.
Learn and grow rapidly, with opportunities to take on more responsibilities as you gain experience.
Play a key role in the development of One Eleven beyond just coding, helping us innovate and evolve our services.


Required Skills:

Good understanding of programming concepts.
Strong desire to learn and adapt to new technologies and development practices.
Ability to work independently and take ownership of your learning and projects.
Good problem-solving skills and attention to detail.


What We Offer:

A Unique Opportunity: Be part of a team where your contributions directly impact the growth and success of the company. We are a small team, meaning you’ll have a significant influence on the direction we take.
Growth & Development: You’ll have the chance to lead projects with full ownership, learn new technologies, and take on responsibilities that go beyond traditional development roles.
Creative Freedom: Your ideas matter. We value creativity and encourage you to bring your unique perspective to the table.
Global Impact: Work with startups from around the world, gaining exposure to different industries and challenges.
A Fun, Supportive Environment: Join a team that values collaboration, innovation, and having fun while doing what we love.


Qualifications: 
We are looking for someone with a problem-solving background and experience in writing code. Ideally, you’ll have a BEng or BSc with a major in Computer Science, but any qualification that involves problem-solving and coding is welcome.
